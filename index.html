Suffixes for possessed and non-possessed nouns

In the possession construction in Yawarana, the possessor noun occurs immediately preceding the possessed noun, which is the head of the possession phrase. â—ï¸ Alternatively, the possessor can appear as a prefix on the possessed noun.
